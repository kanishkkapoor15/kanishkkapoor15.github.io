Ever wondered how banks work, let us show you how!

Banks offer deposit accounts that are secure places for people to keep their money. Banks use the money in deposit accounts to make loans to other people or businesses. In return, the bank receives interest payments on those loans from borrowers. Banks provide interest to the people who have money in deposit accounts!
